Towards the north-eastern segment of the ring you can find Pacifica. Oddly enough, it is a seaside region; despite the drought elsewhere. An egalitarian nation who falls to neither Technology nor Magic - remaining a neutral force. The population is filled with very capable individuals, so much so that the other nations are constantly trying to recruit them. The pacifist nature of the citizens sometimes makes recruitment difficult.

Egalitarian based on a seaside region. Neutral party in the tech v magic war. They have a lot of capable people, so other kingdoms are trying to recruit them. Pacifists. Pacifica.
