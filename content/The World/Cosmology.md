The Cosmos are an ever-present source of mystery and intrigue. Venia is no different.  

It is a common belief that the land of Venia was once populated with, and overseen by, a plethora of Gods. Nobody has any proof, at least none that they've shared publicly. 

An immense comet has frequently been seen as far back as anybody can remember. There are eye witnesses, crude drawings, and mentions of it in ancient texts. Despite all of this 'exposure', nobody knows anything about it. Does it contain magic? Technology? Both? Neither? Nobody knows for sure, however the new Obtatia visitors seem to be highly interested in it.
