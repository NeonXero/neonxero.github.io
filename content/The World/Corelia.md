An agrarian nation that has a not-so-hidden class system. Those in the upper class get to enjoy the fruits of the great magics that have been discovered, practiced, and learned through the years. The lower class gets to work amongst a plethora of fantastical farm creatures. They are forbidden from using magic; however they are afforded the user of simple technology to work their farm equipment.

Agrarian country with distinct class system; upper and land-workers. Upper class = magic. Lower class = forbidden to use, but they have basic farming gear. Critters; fantastical farm creatures. Corellia
