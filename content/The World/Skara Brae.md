Skara Brae, a land filled to the brim with chaos. It is home to everyone who does not have a home. The capital, Durtyville, is obviously corrupted by the group known as the Devil Dogz. Outside of this corruption is no haven, however. The entirety of Skara Brae is viewed by many as a lawless wasteland. The citizens steal both magical and technological equipment and knowledge from the other nations. Whenever the question arises of why there is a war going on, Skara Brae always finds it's way into the list of answers.

Skara Brae. Get the outcasts from other kingdoms. They’re the reason why others fight. Devil Dogz. Steal tech and magic, have good and bad folks. Not much law - kill or be killed. Melting pot. Durtyville.
