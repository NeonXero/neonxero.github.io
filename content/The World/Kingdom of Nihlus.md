Nihlus, located near Tentia, but further to the west. The nation is split geographically across the great divide, though very much together as a culture. The strange astronomical phenomenon that has grown from the cracking of the ring is a great source of inspiration for the citizens of Nihlus. Some say it looks like a star, some say a swirling mist of color, and others describe it just as a light, but are unable to assign characteristics to it. Many have made it their life's goal to understand why it appears differently to everyone who gazes upon it. Their interest lies in magical advances, but technology is utilized in this endeavor.

Magic Kingdom - part of the ring that has a great view into the sky. Nebula or other phenomenon, drawing inspiration from. Nihlus. People in robes, celestial chaps, technology in the interest of furthering their magic.
