The new kids on the block, Obtatia. They refer to themselves as a "Sacred Order", though much of the world has not been made aware of this fact (yet). They have not approached any of the Technologic or Magical counsel, so they remain neutral in everyone's eyes. This neutrality is a point of confusion due to passersby noticing they are amassing a small military. There also seems to be quite a lot of mining gear, noises, and rumbles in the nearby area. As of yet, the leader of this nation has not been identified. There are rumors of cult-like behavior and deep fascination with the nearby celestial bodies.
