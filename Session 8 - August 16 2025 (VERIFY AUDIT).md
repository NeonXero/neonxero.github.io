# Recap

> [!NOTE]- Recap 1
> Sycamore communed with some spirits of long-lost creatures in an attempt to gather some information on the party's first villain - [[The Collector Of Lost Souls]]
> She learns that The Collector has an army 10 thousand strong, who follow their leader blindly. This massive army is comprised of misfits, outsiders, and orphans. They wanted to be found. The Collector found them.
> This force is working towards reaching THE CORE - they are after the rare treasures that are housed there, according to the legends.

> Tidus was curious about whether or not this army of misfit creatures is related in any way to the feud of Magic vs. Technology in the land of Venia. For better or worse, The Collector and his army do not care for such things, they are simply interested in raiding The Core.

> [!NOTE]- Recap 4
> Our party now has control over the main castle in Tentia. This castle is home to a hearty food storage, a classy dining room, a fancy ballroom, and a spacious and modern great hall. The staff from before the captivity situation still reside there, happy to help keep the castle in order.
> 
> In addition to this castle, Glomp and friends put out numerous flyers in an attempt to hire some new talent for around town.
> The lucky applicants included
> 1. Lil Payne - Tentia's favorite weapon shop owner.
> 2. McNibbins, the gnome underwriter - hopefully you're not into any risky business.
> 3. Plink, the puppet accountant - Their big eyes and red hair are almost as fantastical as their bookkeeping skills.
> 4. Doug Dripple, the sentient water - Anything you give him has a high chance of being turned into a potion. 
> 5. Lump, the towering ten-foot human - They never need a ladder to reach the books on the top shelf.
> 6. Brigita Human, definitely not the Oracle from the Matrix - Always ready with a sweet treat, butterscotch is her specialty. Just make sure you get some sweets before she eats them all. Seamstress / medic.
